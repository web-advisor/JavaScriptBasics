Pixel++ Calculator

Pixel

Defined as smallest addressable screen element.Each pixel has it's own intensity, defined by Color Depth of the pixel. Pixel is measure of screen resolution.

Aspect Ratio

Ratio of Width(horizontal point) to Height(vertical point) required to produce equal equal length lines in both the direction on the screen.

Color Depth

It decides the number of colors available for a Pixel.Also called Bit Depth .Unit Bits per Pixel.

Size

Size of a File, Image,Frame Buffer is equal to the product of Resolution and Color Depth.
